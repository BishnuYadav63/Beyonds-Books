    A Few Words About Books

    It is wisely said that books are our first best friends or companions. They are our best friends because they demand nothing from us and help us learn various lessons of life. They give us plenty of joy and amusement. They help us in taking into different world and imagination. They help in making out outlook broad. They also help in our education in various fields. They help to add to our knowledge. They also help us in sharpening the intellect. They provide us with various experiences from different corners of the world. One should develop the habit of reading healthy books. We also must select our they wisely and carefully.They encourage us when we are defeated they gives us the real pleasure of life. When we read good book, we forget ourselves. They, magazines and journals do not bore us. They make our life happy, they give us plenty of joy.
